## ğŸ“ IMPORTANT CONCEPTS

### PRIMARY Products
Products with BOTH brand content AND pricing/SKU from distributor.  
These are the most valuable - full information!

### SECONDARY Products
Products from brand website but NOT yet in distributor system.  
Complete descriptions but no pricing yet.

### HALILIT_ONLY Products
Accessories, cases, pedals, and legacy products only in distributor.  
Good for ecosystem completeness but not primary instruments.

### Match Score
Confidence that a brand product matched correctly with distributor item.  
Range: 0-100%. We require 85%+ for PRIMARY status.

## ğŸ†˜ TROUBLESHOOTING

### "No products found"
- Check internet connection
- Verify scrape_config.json exists
- Check if website URL changed
- Review logs in `backend/logs/`

### "Low PRIMARY products (30%)"
- Brand website might have limited catalog
- Check if distributor has more items
- This is OK - SECONDARY products still useful

### "Duplicate products appearing"
- Matching threshold might be too low
- Check match scores in unified catalog
- Contact admin if duplicates persist

### "Automation not running"
- Check cron: `crontab -l | grep Ecosystem`
- Verify logs exist: `ls -la backend/logs/`
- Test manually: `python scripts/ecosystem_orchestrator.py --brand=nord`
